Baud Rate determines serial communication speed between a controller and DYNAMIXELs.

| Value     | Baud Rate     |
| :-------: | :-----------: |
|8|10.5M|
|7|4.5M|
|6|4M|
|5|3M|
|4|2M|
|3|1M|
|2|115,200|
|1(Default)|57,600|
|0|9,600|

**NOTE** : Less than 3% of the baud rate error margin will not affect to UART communication.
{: .notice}
